Quick guides

Prepare a device for repair

  1. Back up data where possible (cloud or external drive).
  2. Power off and remove any SIM or SD cards.
  3. Observe anti-static precautions — work on a grounded surface.

Reference: iFixit safety & teardown guides: iFixit — Introduction to device repair
