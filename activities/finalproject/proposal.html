1. Title and Topic of My Site

Title: Game Hutt Tech Services

Topic: A website showcasing my electronics repair business for students, focusing on fast, affordable, and multilingual tech repair services for phones, laptops, and speakers.

Why I Chose This Topic:

I chose this topic because I want to share my passion for fixing electronics and show how students can benefit from affordable, reliable repair services. This project allows me to combine my technical skills, personal experience running Game Hutt Tech Services, and website development knowledge. I also want to raise awareness of the importance of repairing electronics instead of throwing them away, which helps students save money and reduces electronic waste.

2. Content on Home Page and Five Pages

  • Home Page: Introduction, services overview, business hours, contact info, and a small slideshow of repairs.
  • Services Page: Detailed description of phone, laptop, and speaker repair services, pricing, and turnaround times.
  • About Page: Story of how I started Game Hutt Tech Services, mission, and goals.
  • Form Page: Contact and service request form for students to submit repair requests.
  • Gallery & Table Page: Gallery of repaired electronics and a table showing service types, prices, and estimated repair times.
