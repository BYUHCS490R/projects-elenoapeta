The Icon of San Francisco

The Golden Gate Bridge is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Built in 1937, this bright orange suspension bridge connects San Francisco to Marin County. It’s not just an engineering marvel—it’s also a symbol of creativity and strength.
